School, School, and More School

After looking back through my adult years, I have realized that I have really been through a crazy amount of schooling. I am about to take you on a trip of where I have gone to school, when I was there, and my purpose for going.



St. Charles Community College (STCC)

1998-1999

I was fresh out of high school with absolutely no idea of where I wanted to go in life.I decided to go STCC for a year, take some basic classics, and try to figure out what I wanted to major in and what college I should transfer to after the year.



University of Missouri-Columbia (Mizzou)

1999-2001

I had finally decided to major in Interior Design and Mizzou had a really good Interior Design program. I went here for 2 years and in the middle of my 2nd year, I decided that this really wasn't for me and switched to Graphic Design. After researching their Graphics program, I was disappointed. Almost none of the classes were computer based and it was going to take me 3 more years to finish, so I moved on.



Hickey College

2001-2002

I looked into every option for Graphics and decided to try out Hickey College. They offered a one year certificate program and I was really impressed with what they offered. After I completed the program I was able to get a job in graphic design right away and have been working in the graphics field for the past 15 years. This school taught me so much more than anything I had learned in the previous three years of college.



St. Charles Community College (STCC)

2011-2013

Yep, I'm back at STCC. After years of graphics I realized the job market was tough and that the pay wasn't very good. I decided to go back to school and finish my degree hoping this would increase my chances of getting noticed when applyig for a new job. My plan here is to finish a few mandatory classes before signing up for Mizzou-Online to complete my bachelor's degree.



University of Missouri-Columbia (Mizzou)-Online

2013-2016

During these years I'm taking classes, all while my personal life has led me to meet someone, marry him, have 2 kids, and unfortunately also get a divorce. It was a busy couple of years and I wasn't able to finish, it just became too much to take on at that point in time.



St. Charles Community College

2016

Haha...one more time! At this point in time I say forget the bachelor's degree. Just give me anything to put on a resume. I returned to STCC and they put it together that I could get an associates degree. I technically didn't have to take any classes, but still had to apply. But, finally, after all this time, I HAVE A DEGREE!



LaunchCode

2017-present

After my divorce, I realized that graphics wasn't going to be enough to support me and my two kids...plus I was really burnt out in graphic design. After looking around, I stumbled across LaunchCode and decided it was fate that brought me here. I'm hoping to really go places after learning computer programming.
